Visual School Portal

Visual School Portal (VSP) is a smart school platform designed for ASUBEB primary schools pupils in Anambra State to have direct access to their results and overall performance anytime and anywhere using a smartphones, laptop or desktop. This shift to a digital platform is not merely a technological evolution, but a required change brought by the advent of the internet, the way people collaborate with information today thereby providing visibility of these schools.

About Us

We are the future of Anambra State Education

Anambra State Universal Basic Education Board (ASUBEB) is a body that overseas the affairs of primary schools in the state with the support of Federal Government of Nigeria through Universal Basic Education Commission (UBEC). Over the years UBEC has been supporting primary schools across the nation with standard trainings for teachers, good infrastructures for conducive learning environment and free text books supply. ASUBEB invited an ICT organisation MicNet Concept International to assist them in providing solutions for online data retrieval and online results to enable parents/guidance have direct access to their ward results and overall performance anytime and anywhere using a smartphones, laptop or desktop.
